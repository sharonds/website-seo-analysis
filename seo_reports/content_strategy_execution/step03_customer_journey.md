## Journey Summary: Cross-Stage Insights

### Universal Search Patterns:
1. **Language Evolution:** Searches become more sophisticated and specific as customers progress
2. **Trust Building:** Increasing focus on credentials, reviews, and social proof
3. **Cost Sensitivity:** Persistent concern about fees across all stages and personas
4. **Urgency Indicators:** Time-sensitive language increases pressure at each stage
5. **Location Relevance:** Local search becomes important at decision stage

### Content Strategy Implications:
- **Awareness Content:** Basic rights explanations, eligibility guides, common triggers
- **Consideration Content:** Success stories, process explanations, fee comparisons
- **Decision Content:** Contact information, preparation guides, consultation process

### SEO Priority Keywords:
- High-volume: "ביטוח סיעוד", "עורך דין ביטוח סיעוד"
- Long-tail: "שיעור הצלחה עורכי דין ביטוח סיעוד פרקינסון"
- Local: "עורך דין ביטוח סיעוד נתניה/תל אביב"
- Urgent: "חברת ביטוח דחתה תביעה מה לעשות"
